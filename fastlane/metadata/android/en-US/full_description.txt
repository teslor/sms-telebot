SMS Telebot forwards important phone events from your device to Telegram bot or email via SMTP

Use it to stay informed when your phone receives:
- SMS messages
- Incoming calls
- Low battery alerts
- Charger connection updates

Why choose SMS Telebot:
- Flexible forwarding rules: create multiple rules and route events to different Telegram bots or SMTP recipients
- Powerful filtering: filter by sender or message text with support for regular expressions
- Whitelist and blacklist modes: include only matching events or exclude matched ones
- Always-on background mode: improved reliability on devices with aggressive battery/background restrictions
- Automatic retries: failed forwarding attempts are retried when temporary issues (for example, no internet) are resolved
- Secure credential storage: bot tokens and SMTP passwords are stored in encrypted form

Important:
- For stable delivery, exclude the app from battery optimization on your device
- Some Android vendors apply stricter background limits, so behavior may vary by firmware
- Make sure required permissions (SMS/phone/notifications) are granted
